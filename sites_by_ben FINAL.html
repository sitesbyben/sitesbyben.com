Common Questions

FAQ

Have a question not answered here? Reach out — I'm happy to chat.

Ask a Question →
How long does a typical project take?+
Most projects go from kickoff to launch in 1–2 weeks. Larger or more complex sites can take 2–4 weeks. I'll always give you a realistic timeline upfront before we start.
How much does a website cost?+
Every project is quoted individually based on scope. I provide a clear, flat-rate quote after our consultation — no hourly billing, no surprise charges.
Do I need to provide content and images?+
It helps if you have content ready, but it's not required. I can help guide you on what to write and suggest high-quality stock photography if needed.
Will I be able to update the site myself?+
Yes — if you want that ability, I build your site on a CMS that makes updates easy, even without technical knowledge. Or I can handle all updates via a maintenance plan.
What if I already have a domain and hosting?+
No problem. I can work with your existing setup or help you choose better hosting if needed. I'll handle all the technical migration and configuration.
Do you offer payment plans?+
Yes. Standard projects are split 50% upfront and 50% at launch. For larger projects, other arrangements can be discussed. I'm flexible and want to make it easy to work together.
